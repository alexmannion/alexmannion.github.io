About


I'm an aspiring 3D artist with a background in videography and computer aided design.

Before attending Champlain College, I had little to no artistic experience, and didn't even consider 3D modeling art. My view has certainly changed since then. I have become intimately familiar with how much passion, creativity and drive is required to become a good 3D artist, especially within 4 years. In my first year at this college I realized I had a lot of catching up to do, and made sure to stay practiced and make use of that few advantages I had. The main one being, my love for programming and understanding the technical side of game development. I'd originally applied to Champlain as a Game Designer, but my girlfriend at the time made me realize that what I really wanted to be was a 3D artist.

Since then I've been keeping in touch with my roots as a programmer, while mostly focusing on advancing my artistic ability, and pushing myself wherever possible. Unfortunately I'm not a machine, and I need to take breaks from 3D modeling sometimes. In my free time recently, I've been learning French and playing a lot of tennis. I also have been playing guitar and drums for a couple years, and love movies and television as well.
Some of my favorite shows and movies are Malcolm in The Middle, Lost, Twin Peaks, Merlin, everything Monty Python, and the Lord of The Rings movies (Extended Editions).
